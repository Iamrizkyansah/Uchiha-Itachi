UCHIHA ITACHI

The prodigy who sacrificed everything for peace, carrying the burden of his choices alone to protect the village and his beloved brother.

JUTSU

Master of the Mangekyō Sharingan, Itachi possessed some of the most powerful and unique techniques in the Naruto universe.

月

Tsukuyomi

A genjutsu that traps the target in an illusionary world completely controlled by the caster. Time, space, and physical laws can be manipulated at will.

Type Genjutsu
Rank S-Class

Abilities

  • Complete control over time perception
  • Tortures the target mentally for days in seconds
  • Can only be broken by someone with Sharingan
天

Amaterasu

Black flames that materialize at the focal point of the user's vision. The flames burn until their target is completely incinerated and cannot be extinguished by normal means.

Type Ninjutsu
Rank S-Class

Abilities

  • Black flames that never extinguish
  • Burns through almost anything
  • Can be shaped with Susanoo
須

Susanoo

A gigantic humanoid avatar made of the user's chakra that surrounds and protects them. Itachi's Susanoo wields the Totsuka Blade and Yata Mirror.

Type Kekkei Genkai
Rank S-Class

Abilities

  • Near-impenetrable defense with Yata Mirror
  • Seals anything it pierces with Totsuka Blade
  • Complete protection from all directions
伊

Izanami

A genjutsu that traps the target in an endless loop of a specific moment, created to punish those who abuse the power of Izanagi. It can only be escaped by accepting one's fate.

Type Genjutsu
Rank Forbidden

Abilities

  • Traps target in an infinite time loop
  • Created to counter Izanagi
  • Causes user to lose sight in one eye
鴉

Crow Clone

A variation of the Shadow Clone technique where the clones are made of crows. When destroyed, the crows can reform or be used for other purposes.

Type Ninjutsu
Rank B-Class

Abilities

  • Clones disperse into crows when hit
  • Crows can be used for genjutsu
  • Can reform after being dispersed
火

Fire Style

Itachi was a master of Fire Release techniques, capable of performing powerful fire-based attacks without hand signs at a young age.

Type Ninjutsu
Rank Various

Techniques

  • Fireball Jutsu - Master level
  • Phoenix Flower Jutsu
  • Dragon Fire Jutsu

STORY

The tragic tale of a prodigy who carried the weight of the world on his shoulders, sacrificing everything for peace.

Age 4

The Child Prodigy

Itachi awakened his Sharingan at the tender age of 4 after witnessing the horrors of the Third Great Ninja War. His genius-level intellect and exceptional skills quickly made him renowned throughout the village.

"Those who cannot acknowledge themselves will eventually fail."
Age 7

Graduation from Academy

Itachi graduated from the Ninja Academy at the top of his class after only one year. He mastered the basics of ninjutsu, taijutsu, and genjutsu with unprecedented speed.

"Growth occurs when one goes beyond one's limits. Realizing that is also part of training."
Age 11

ANBU Membership

At age 11, Itachi joined the ANBU Black Ops, becoming the youngest member in Konoha's history. He was assigned to monitor the Uchiha clan's movements as tensions grew.

"Knowledge and awareness are vague, and perhaps better called illusions."
Age 13

The Uchiha Massacre

Faced with an impossible choice between his clan and the village, Itachi chose to eliminate the Uchiha clan to prevent a civil war, sparing only his younger brother Sasuke.

"No matter how powerful you become, do not try to shoulder everything alone. If you do, you will fail."
Age 14-21

Akatsuki Member

Itachi joined the criminal organization Akatsuki to keep watch on them from within and protect Konoha. He partnered with Kisame Hoshigaki while secretly working as a double agent.

"People live their lives bound by what they accept as correct and true. That's how they define reality."
Age 21

The Final Battle & Truth

Itachi orchestrated his final battle with Sasuke, secretly weakened by illness, to die by his brother's hand and transfer his power. The truth about his sacrifices was revealed after his death.

"I will love you always, Sasuke."
